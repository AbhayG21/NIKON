The Nikon D7500 is a high-performance DSLR camera that delivers exceptional image quality and speed. With a 20.9-megapixel sensor, 4K UHD video recording, and 8 fps continuous shooting, it's perfect for capturing stunning photos and videos in various conditions. Its ergonomic design and built-in Wi-Fi make it a versatile choice for enthusiasts and professionals alike.
  • Image Sensor: 20.9MP DX-format CMOS sensor.
  • Processor: EXPEED 5 for fast image processing and 4K video
  • Autofocus: 51-point AF system with 15 cross-type sensors.
  • ISO Range: Versatile ISO from 100 to 51,200 (expandable).
  • Speed: Shoots up to 8 frames per second for fast action capture.
