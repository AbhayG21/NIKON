NIKON
D500
The Nikon D500 is a versatile DSLR camera featuring a 24.2-megapixel sensor and EXPEED 4 image processor, capturing sharp and vibrant photos. With a swiveling 3.2-inch LCD screen, built-in Wi-Fi, and Full HD video recording, it offers creative flexibility for both photography enthusiasts and casual users.
  • Image Sensor: 24.2MP APS-C CMOS sensor for high-resolution images.
  • ISO Range: ISO 100-25600 (expandable to 6400) for versatile low-light shooting.
  • Autofocus: 39-point AF system with 9 cross-type sensors for accurate focusing.
  • Connectivity: Built-in Wi-Fi and Bluetooth for easy image sharing and remote control.
  • LCD Screen: 3.2" vari-angle touchscreen LCD with 1.04M dots for flexible shooting angles.
