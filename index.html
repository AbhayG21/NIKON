NIKON
D5600
The Nikon D5600 is a versatile and user-friendly DSLR camera. With a 24.2-megapixel sensor and EXPEED 4 image processor, it delivers high-quality photos and Full HD videos. Its articulating touchscreen, built-in Wi-Fi, and SnapBridge compatibility enhance creative control and seamless sharing. A great choice for enthusiasts stepping up their photography game.
D3500
The Nikon D3500 is an entry-level DSLR camera known for its user-friendly design and impressive image quality. With a 24.2-megapixel sensor and EXPEED 4 image processor, it offers sharp photos and Full HD videos. Its compact body, helpful Guide Mode, and compatibility with a variety of lenses make it perfect for beginners.
